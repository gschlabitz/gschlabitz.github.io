# Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2 - Notes

## Interesting Places

Visit these to get free gear and food. Names in quotes are the names used on the in-game map.

### Troskowitz

- Tree at fork by cemetery: glasses (+26 cha), cheese
- "Target on Tree": Village dogwood bow, arrows, Lepiota farm (every 3 days?)
- "Apple Orchard": Apple farm (every 3 days?)
- "Bench under Trosky": Speech book
- "Overgrown dugout": Chest key is in pot by entrance, belladonna
- "Abandoned beehives": Honey farm (Last harvest: 274 in-game hours)
- Beehives near hunters lodge: Honey farm (Last harvest: 272.9 in-game hours)
- Across road from ? farm: Belladonna farm

### Selling

- Trader pays less than specialized merchants
- Scribe: books, maps
- Blacksmith: weapons
- Taylor: armor, clothes
- Woodcutter: bows
